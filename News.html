Space odyssey: New Horizons completes historic, three-billion-mile journey to Pluto

by Hub staff report

July 14, 2015

NASA's New Horizons spacecraft has passed Pluto. After a decade-long journey through our solar system, New Horizons made its closest approach to Pluto on Tuesday morning at approximately 7:49 a.m. EDT, about 7,750 miles above the surface—roughly the same distance from New York to Mumbai, India—making it the first-ever space mission to explore a world so far from Earth.
