Robotic Telemanipulation with Time Delay ("Satellite Surgery")

This research project in the Sensing, Manipulation, and Real-Time Systems lab is aiming to develop methods for telerobotic on-orbit servicing of spacecraft under ground-based supervisory control of human operators.

People/Location:
Peter Kazanzides/Laboratory for Computational Sensing and Robotics

Nanosatellite Guidance, Navigation, and Control

This work focuses on guidance and control of small satellites such as cubesats performing proximity operations in a several meters range of a target object. The main goal is to develop a principled methodology for handling the coupled effects of orbital dynamics, rotational and translational rigid body dynamics, underactuation and control bounds, and obstacle avoidance constraints.

People/Location:
Marin Kobilarov/Laboratory for Computational Sensing and Robotics

Robotic Lunar Lander

The International Lunar Network (ILN) will establish a network of robotic geophysical monitoring stations on the surface of the Moon in order to improve our understanding of lunar interior structure and composition.

People/Location:

NEAR Shoemaker

Near Earth Asteroid Rendezvous (NEAR) Shoemaker was a robotic space probe that conducted in-depth studies of the near-Earth asteroids 433-Eros and Mathilde. It was the first spacecraft to orbit and land on an asteroid.
